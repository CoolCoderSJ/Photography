About Me

I like to take pictures of nature or any nice scenery that I come across. I like to mostly do nature photography, because I find a lot of beauty in nature. My favorite camera mode is the manual mode, because with manual mode I get full control over the camera and get to decide how I take my pictures. My hobbies include photography, collecting souvenir pennies, reading, and programming.
